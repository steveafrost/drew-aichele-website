I have always had a passion for baking and cooking. Since a child, I have had the goal for pursuing this passion. I am always looking for more opportunities to learn and grow in the culinary world. In addition, I am currently pursuing work as a Food Stylist Assistant.

One of the happiest days of my culinary career was creating my sister’s wedding cake. This particular style was a naked vanilla lemon cake with a cheesecake layer, graham cracker pie dough crumb, and raspberry buttercream frosting. After the event, I posted a picture of it to Reddit's most popular food section, reddit.com/r/food, and received an incredible response.
